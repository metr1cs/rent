# Admin Moderation SLA

## Scope
- Venue cards moderation
- Reviews moderation (`pending` queue)
- Disputes and complaints handling

## SLA Targets
- Review moderation queue:
  - First touch: <= 4 hours
  - Final status decision: <= 24 hours
- Venue card moderation:
  - New/updated card decision: <= 24 hours
- Disputes:
  - First response: <= 24 hours
  - Resolution: <= 5 business days

## Roles
- Moderator L1:
  - Processes standard pending reviews/cards
  - Applies templates and standard actions
- Moderator L2 / Lead:
  - Handles high-risk and escalations
  - Reviews edge cases and disputes
- Product/Legal:
  - Final policy decisions for contentious cases

## Daily Process
1. Open moderation dashboard 3 times/day.
2. Prioritize:
   - high-risk pending reviews
   - oldest pending over SLA
3. Apply action with reason/note.
4. Escalate disputed or high-impact cases.

## Weekly Control
- KPI review:
  - pending backlog
  - `pendingOverSla`
  - median time to moderation
  - false-positive / false-negative feedback from support
