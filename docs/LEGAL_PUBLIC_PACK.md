# Legal Public Pack (VmestoRu)

Public pages required in production:
- `/privacy` — Privacy Policy
- `/terms` — Public Offer / User Agreement
- `/disputes` — Disputes & Complaints Regulation

## Publish Rules
- Links to all three pages must be in footer.
- Last updated date should be tracked in internal release notes.
- Any legal text change requires Product + Legal sign-off before release.

## Minimal Compliance Controls
- Contact channels must be explicit (`email`, `phone`, support form).
- Data processing legal basis must be listed.
- User rights and request process must be documented.
- Complaint and moderation process must be transparent.
